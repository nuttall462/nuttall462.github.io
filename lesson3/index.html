Site Purpose

It's the epitome of small talk; "Nice weather we're having today." Here at Whether the Weather, we take this common ground that everyone understands to bring the community together. Not only will you find the most reliable weather reports on our website, but we strive to keep you as informed as possible about local news, events, gatherings, and so much more. As you browse our website, we hope that our purpose shows through and we encourage you to give us feedback if it is less than satisfactory. We're all under one big sky, so let's enjoy it together.
